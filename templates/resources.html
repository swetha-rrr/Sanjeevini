Finding Help When You Need It

We've assembled a comprehensive collection of support services and organizations dedicated to women's safety and wellbeing. Whether you're in an emergency situation or looking for ongoing support, these resources are here to help.

Emergency Helplines

Women's Helpline

1091

24/7 national helpline for women in distress. Immediate assistance available in multiple languages.

Call Now

Domestic Violence

181

For reporting domestic violence cases and getting immediate intervention and protection.

Call Now

Police Emergency

100

For immediate police assistance in emergency situations requiring urgent intervention.

Call Now

Ambulance

108

For medical emergencies requiring immediate medical attention and transportation.

Call Now

Support Organizations

National Commission for Women

Government body dedicated to protecting and promoting women's interests and rights. Provides legal assistance, policy advocacy, and grievance resolution.

Visit Website

Shakti Shalini

NGO supporting survivors of gender-based violence through legal aid, counseling, shelter, and rehabilitation services. Available 24/7 for crisis intervention.

Visit Website

All India Women's Conference

Works for women's education, social welfare, and empowerment through various programs including legal awareness camps and vocational training.

Additional Support Services

Legal Aid Services

Free legal assistance for women facing violence, harassment, or discrimination. Includes consultation, document preparation, and court representation.

Find Legal Help

Safe Shelters Network

Emergency accommodation for women and children fleeing dangerous situations. Provides food, clothing, and connections to long-term support.

Locate Shelters

Counseling Services

Professional mental health support specialized in trauma, abuse recovery, and building resilience. Available both online and in-person.
