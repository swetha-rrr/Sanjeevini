Safety Tips

Personal Safety

  • Always keep emergency contacts saved and easily accessible
  • Share your location with trusted friends or family when traveling
  • Use our GPS tracker when in unfamiliar areas
  • Consider carrying a personal safety device or whistle
  • Be aware of your surroundings and trust your instincts

Commuting Safety

  • Use registered public transport or verified cab services
  • Check if the vehicle and driver details match before boarding
  • Share your trip details with someone you trust
  • Avoid isolated routes, especially after dark
  • Keep your phone charged and accessible

Digital Safety

  • Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
  • Be cautious about sharing personal information online
  • Review privacy settings on social media regularly
  • Report online harassment immediately
  • Be careful with location sharing on apps and social media

Workplace Safety

  • Familiarize yourself with workplace sexual harassment policies
  • Know your rights and the reporting procedures
  • Document inappropriate incidents or behaviors
  • Support colleagues who report harassment
  • Contact the Internal Complaints Committee if needed
